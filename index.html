Welcome to AlzheScan

"Alzheimer's Severity Classifier"

AlzheScan is an innovative application designed to help you quickly and accurately detect and classify the severity of Alzheimer's. The classification results will be divided into 4 classes, namely Non-Demented, Mild-Demented, Moderate-Demented, and VeryMild-Demented. Using the EfficientNetB7 model, we provide reliable results to aid in medical decision making.
